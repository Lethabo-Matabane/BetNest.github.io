About BetNest Cafe

Our Story

Founded: 10 March 2020 — Location: Mabopane. BetNest Cafe began as a small neighbourhood internet cafe and quickly evolved into a mixed-use destination combining study space, high-speed internet, and a dedicated gaming zone. Over the years we’ve hosted local tournaments, helped students with CVs and job applications, and become a reliable hangout for gamers and remote workers alike.

Mission

To provide exceptional gaming and professional experiences within a safe and welcoming environment that supports learning, competition and community connection.

Vision

To grow BetNest into the leading local hub for gamers, students and freelancers — known for fast internet, well-maintained equipment, and memorable community events.

Target Audience

  • Gamers aged 11–30 (casual and competitive)
  • Adults 18+ who use the cafe for tournaments and social gaming
  • Students and job-seekers needing internet, printing and CV help
  • Parents looking for a safe, supervised place for their children

What We Do

BetNest provides:

  • High-speed internet (hourly / daily / monthly packages)
  • PC rigs and PlayStation consoles for hourly use and tournaments
  • Printing, scanning and basic computer help
  • CV preparation assistance and career resources
  • Regular community tournaments with prizes and member discounts
